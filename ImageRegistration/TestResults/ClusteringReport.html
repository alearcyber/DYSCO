Report on Clustering

This document discusses some of the rudimentary clustering that was done on the law's texture features. The code where this is done is located in TextureEnergy.py

The hope is that the textures present in the area of the obstruction will be separable by clustering. THe thought being there should be 2 unique clusters: the obstruction textures and the unobstructed textures.

The original image was a picture of a display failure. It can be seen below. No relevant preprocessing was done to the image apart from correcting the orientation.


What was done

The 5 texture vectors were multiplied in a pairwise manner to create 25 texture filters of shape 5 by 5. The image was convolved with the 25 filters at each color channel to create a 75 layer image of textures. Below is the cumulative variance of the first 15 principle components.
